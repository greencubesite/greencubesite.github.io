What is GreenCube?

The Green Cube is a vending machine kit representing a flexible solution that combines already established Electric Vehicle Charging Stations with eco-friendly components which bring the customer additional convenience and a sense of social responsibility. Our idea is to place different types of vending machines and recycling stations that work in congruence with each other. Our services will be provided on strategic locations with high consumer downtime.
